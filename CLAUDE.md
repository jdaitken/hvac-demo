# HVAC Demo Site - Project Specification

## Project Overview

This is a conversion-focused demo website for Ottawa HVAC contractors. The site demonstrates how a professional web presence generates leads 24/7 through strategic design, local SEO optimization, and clear calls-to-action.

## Business Context

- **Target audience:** Local HVAC contractors in Ottawa who need online presence
- **Demo purpose:** Show tangible value of $275-375/month web service
- **Key selling point:** This site captures leads while contractors are on service calls

## Technical Stack

- **Framework:** Astro (static site generation)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **Deployment:** SiteGround hosting
- **Domain:** TBD (subdomain of jdaitken.ca or separate demo domain)

## Site Structure

### 1. Homepage

- Hero section with emergency contact CTA (large, prominent)
- Service area map showing Ottawa coverage
- 3-4 key services (furnace, AC, maintenance, emergency)
- Social proof section (testimonials, years in business, certifications)
- Secondary CTA for free estimates

### 2. Services Page

- Residential services section
- Commercial services section
- Emergency services highlight
- Seasonal messaging (current: winter/furnace focus)
- Clear pricing transparency or "request quote" CTAs

### 3. About Page

- Local business trust-building
- Family-owned/community-focused angle
- Team photo placeholder
- Certifications and licenses
- Why choose us section

### 4. Contact/Booking Page

- Simple lead capture form (name, phone, email, service needed, preferred date)
- Multiple contact methods (phone, email, form)
- Service area confirmation
- Business hours
- Emergency contact option

### 5. Blog Section (2-3 sample posts)

- "When to Replace Your Furnace in Ottawa Winters"
- "AC Maintenance Checklist for Ottawa Homeowners"
- "How to Choose an HVAC Contractor in Ottawa"
- Purpose: Demonstrate SEO value and content strategy

## Design Direction

### Brand (Generic/Neutral)

- **Business name:** "Superior HVAC Ottawa" (or similar professional name)
- **Color scheme:** Professional blues/grays with warm orange accent for urgency CTAs
- **Typography:** Clean, readable, professional but approachable
- **Imagery:** Use placeholder images that suggest local Ottawa homes/businesses

### Key Design Principles

- **Mobile-first:** HVAC emergencies happen on phones
- **Click-to-call:** Every page needs prominent phone number
- **Trust signals:** Certifications, testimonials, local focus throughout
- **Urgency:** Emergency service availability clear on every page
- **Simplicity:** Homeowners in crisis need simple, clear paths to contact

## Conversion Elements

- Emergency contact button (sticky on mobile)
- Lead capture form with minimal friction
- Clear service area messaging (Ottawa + surrounding areas)
- Social proof strategically placed
- Multiple contact options (some prefer call, some prefer form)

## Content Strategy

- Focus on Ottawa-specific context (winter heating, summer cooling)
- Emphasize 24/7 availability and emergency response
- Local business language ("serving Ottawa families since...")
- Educational content shows expertise without overwhelming

## Success Metrics (To Demonstrate to Contractors)

- Clear contact options on every page
- Fast load times (under 2 seconds)
- Mobile-responsive design
- Local SEO optimization (Ottawa keywords)
- Easy-to-update blog for ongoing SEO

## Development Priorities

1. Core page structure and navigation
2. Homepage with strong hero and CTAs
3. Contact form functionality
4. Services page with clear offerings
5. About page for trust-building
6. Blog template with sample posts
7. Mobile optimization
8. Performance optimization

## Notes for Claude Code

- Keep code clean and well-commented for future client customization
- Use reusable components (button styles, form elements, CTAs)
- Ensure easy color scheme swapping for client branding
- Build with maintenance in mind (this is a recurring revenue model)
